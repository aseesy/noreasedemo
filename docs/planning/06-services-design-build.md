# Design-Build Construction Services

**URL:** https://noreasinc.com/services/design-build-construction/

---

## Service Overview

NOREAS offers full-service engineering design and construction services for remediation as well as clean construction projects. The company is a licensed, general engineering and construction contractor with a bonding capability of up to $15 million.

---

## Design Capabilities

The design team includes:
- Civil engineers
- Mechanical engineers
- Structural engineers
- Electrical engineers

They provide horizontal and vertical design services for both new construction, as well as renovation and upgrading of existing infrastructure.

---

## In-House Construction Services

NOREAS handles design-build and construction services for:
- Buildings
- Roads
- Subgrade preparation
- Foundations
- Sewer and water conveyance systems
- Building abatement and demolition

---

## Project Scope

### Infrastructure
- Roads, parking lots, and pavement work
- Electrical system upgrades and street lighting
- Heavy civil construction (grading, excavation, drainage)
- Utility installation and relocation

### Buildings & Structures
- Concrete, masonry, and structural systems
- Secondary containment and environmental covers
- HVAC and plumbing systems
- Building demolition and new construction

### Environmental/Remediation
- Soil excavation
- Landfill caps
- Brownfields redevelopment
- Site maintenance and turnkey solutions

---

## Oversight Services

Senior staff serve as Owner's Representatives or Resident Engineers, providing:
- Oversight over field activities
- Quality control of construction work
- Progress reporting
- Identification of potential cost saving measures
