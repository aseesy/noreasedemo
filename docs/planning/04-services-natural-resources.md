## Service Overview

NOREAS has provided biology and regulatory permitting services since 2008. The company has assembled biologists, ornithologists, botanists, arborists, herpetologists, mammalogists, ecologists, and related professionals across California, Arizona, and Nevada.

---

## Key Capabilities

### Expertise Areas
- Special-status species survey, inventory, relocation, monitoring, and reporting
- Permitting analysis for more than 500 individual projects affecting wetlands, Waters of the State, and Waters of the U.S.
- Pedestrian-based field surveys on federal, private, and state-managed lands
- GIS analysis and map generation services

### Regulatory Knowledge
The team handles requirements under:
- Section 404 and 401 of the Clean Water Act
- California Fish and Game Code sections (1600, 3500, 2080 et seq.)
- State and Federal Endangered Species Acts
