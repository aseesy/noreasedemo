## Service Overview

NOREAS provides integrated consulting and engineering services by combining effective client communication with appropriate technical expertise. Their approach uses multidisciplinary teams incorporating geologists, hydrogeologists, chemists, microbiologists, engineers, and construction managers.

---

## Environmental Design Areas

### Remediation Technologies

#### Extraction Systems
- **Soil Vapor Extraction (SVE)** - for solvents and petroleum hydrocarbons
- **Air Sparging (AS)** systems
- **Multiphase Extraction (MPE)**
- **Pump and Treat** systems

#### In-Situ Treatment
- **Enhanced In Situ Bioremediation (EISB)** - aerobic/anaerobic implementation
- **In Situ Chemical Oxidation (ISCO)** - using potassium permanganate and activated persulfate
- **In Situ Chemical Reduction (ISCR)** - for hexavalent chromium
- **Permeable Reactive Zones (PRZs)**

#### Monitoring & Assessment
- Biomonitoring using traditional and advanced techniques
- DNAPL investigation and remediation
