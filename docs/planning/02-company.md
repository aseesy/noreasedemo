# NOREAS Inc. - Company

**URL:** https://noreasinc.com/company/

---

## Company Overview

NOREAS Inc. is an engineering and scientific services firm established in **2008**, operating across California, Nevada, and Arizona. The company serves federal agencies, local governments, utility companies, and private industries.

---

## Primary Service Areas

1. Environmental Investigation and Remediation
2. Natural Resources and Regulatory Permitting
3. Engineering and Consulting Services
4. Design-Build Construction
