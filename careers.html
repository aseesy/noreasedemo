Principal Engineer

Irvine, CA Full-time 20+ Years

We are seeking a Principal Engineer with 20+ years of experience in the environmental consulting industry. This position will be based in Irvine, and will require some travel.

Requirements:

  • US citizen or Permanent Resident
  • BS in Civil/Chemical/Mechanical/Environmental Engineering or related field from an accredited university. MS is preferred
  • California PE
  • Current 40 hour HAZWOPER. QCM Training is preferred
  • Experience performing environmental investigation, remediation system design (Pump and Treat, SVE, In Situ Remediation), operations and maintenance (O&M) of remediation systems
  • Experience with DoD projects is preferred
  • Experience with addressing VOC/TPH contamination at complex sites
  • Emerging contaminants and Vapor Intrusion (VI) experience is preferred
  • Experience with the CERCLA process. RCRA experience is preferred
  • Experience in interacting with RWQCB, DTSC and EPA

Key Responsibilities:

  • Preparation of drawings and technical specifications
  • Design of remediation systems, including pump and treat, bioremediation, chemical reduction, thermally-enhanced remediation, SVE, air sparging
  • Remediation system construction management
  • Preparation of work plans and reports
  • Project management
  • Mentoring of junior staff
  • Assist with proposals and business development
