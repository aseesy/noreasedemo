About NOREAS

Engineering Excellence Since 2008

NOREAS Inc. is an engineering and scientific services firm providing comprehensive solutions to federal agencies, local governments, utility companies, and private industries across California, Nevada, and Arizona.

Our team of highly skilled engineers, geologists, environmental scientists, biologists, and construction professionals delivers cost-effective environmental solutions with a proven track record of excellence. We maintain an unrivaled reputation among clients and regulators for addressing complex environmental challenges.
